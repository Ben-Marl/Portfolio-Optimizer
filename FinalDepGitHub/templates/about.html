About Optify

What's with the name?

Optimizes your portfolio , the algorithm behind this application generates 50000 different portfolio distributions and then picks the one from the efficient frontier which has the maximum Sharpe Ratio - a performance measure for investments developed by William F. Sharpe.
Hence the name Optify!
